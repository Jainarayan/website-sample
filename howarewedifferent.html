How We Are Different

Translation, transliteration, transcreation, localization, industrialization, globalization - they are all about language development investment to produce a product that can cross cultural and language barriers to drive revenue, profits and market share in a global market.

We know that you spend every penny to get a high-quality product that can draw in customers and increase your global presence. We also know that a bad-quality product leaves a bad impression and can negatively impact your clients and customer satisfaction and retention.

And that is why we offer our shoulder to be your ladder of success. We strongly believe in having an honest relationship with our client that is based on mutual faith and integrity. Our strong work ethics are based on delivering competent, quality services to our clients. The following unique features of our approach are the backbone of our flourishing business.

Stringent Qualification Process

We identify and finalize resources after doing deep research about their area of expertise, productivity, translation quality, knowledge of tools passed file ratio and feedback. We provide real-time support to the resources during the task to lower the number of doubts and/or queries.

Strong Quality Process

At Audio Bridge, we set a high bar on content quality and have a strong quality process in place to ensure this bar is met. Quality control process starts before assigning the file to the translators, linguist or language experts. After receiving translation from the resources the files are handed over to the reviewers’ team which then follows 6 rigorous QA levels - editing & quality review, reformatting of target file, layout synchronization, proofreading, layout QC and final QC. Quality check is initiated after the translated file(s) has been rendered to the quality supervisors’ team.

24*7 Availability

With our dedicated team’s 24*7 availability, please rest assured that none of your issue/ need will go unaddressed. Our pool of project managers and coordinators are available round the clock to make sure that communication from our end is never delayed.

High Quality at Low Pricing

Our goal is to provide you high-quality service worth every penny that you spend. We offer some of the most competent rates in the industry as we believe in mutual benefits and mutual growth. Our clients’ satisfaction is our joy earned.
