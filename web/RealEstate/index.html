About US

Whether you are buying or selling a home, Ellen Hazo understands the fluctuating emotions behind this journey. Ellen moved to Newburyport thirteen years ago with her husband to raise their family in this hand-picked community. Extensive research into the surrounding public and private schools allows Ellen to share first-hand information to parents who are looking to buy in the Newburyport area . Thru many clubs, groups, sports and personal friends, Ellen has the knowledge and relationships that aid her in achieving her greatest goal - making the home buying process as seamless as possible for her clients.

Ellen shares over twenty years of marketing, sales and volunteer experience with you as she takes proven skills and applies them to positioning your home effectively in the marketplace and, if you are in the market to buy a home, she enlists a keen ear to your wants and needs and has the ability to find and negotiate an efficient delivery for you.

Her philosophy is simple - provide honest and trustworthy information in addition to consistent & attentive service.

Ellen welcomes your communication to explore working together.
